A long time ago, in a datacenter not so far away…

A great darkness had fallen upon the enterprise. Across the known galaxy of home services, sales records were scattered, claims left unadjudicated, and accounting ledgers lost to the void. The Rebellion of manual processes had grown powerful, and no tool could withstand its chaos.

In the year 2018, a small but courageous band of engineers drew their first blueprints in the depths of the Azure cloud. Armed with the ancient arts of Angular and TypeScript, and guided by the Force of Node.js, they began construction of a system that would bring order to the galaxy.

They called it Cogent.

For three years, the engineers labored in secrecy — forging the platform in the fires of iteration, hardening it against the dark side of legacy systems, and breathing into it the power to manage sales, tame accounting ledgers, and render swift justice through claims adjudication.

When the twin suns of 2021 rose over the horizon, COGENT emerged at last from its long incubation. The noble company known as Elevate Homescriptions opened its gates, and the platform descended upon its operations like the Rebel fleet over Endor — precise, powerful, and ready for battle.

Now, running steadfast in the Azure cloud, COGENT stands as the singular CRM of the realm — a New Hope for every sales team, every accountant, every claims officer who ever dared imagine a galaxy where data flows free and no adjudication is left behind.

The Empire of disorder has been defeated. The Force is strong with this platform.

May the code be with you. Always.
